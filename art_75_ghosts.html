Ghosts

(2019, Iphone 6S, Adobe Premier Pro).

Ghosts represents the ghosts of people in CCTV surveillance in the modern world. Our data is stored as data and is forever burned into the memory of the network.

I filmed in perspectives of a CCTV camera and over layed both cuts with opacity on top to create a ghosting effect.

My idea is from the growing prevalence of surveillance cameras in public and private spaces. Its mind blowing how many cameras are installed currently.
