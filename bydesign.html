"PROTEST BY DESIGN"

Exploration and design of gas masks seen in the body of work "PROTEST FASHION: THE CYBERPUNK REVOLUTIONARIES." This body of work draws upon imagery of the infamous 3M respirator that has exploded into the public conciousness as a result of recent political turmoil around the world. (Raymond Lam, Photoshop, Autodesk Maya, After Effects, 2020)
