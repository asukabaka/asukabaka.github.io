"PROTEST FASHION"

Inspiration from recent political turmoil in Hong Kong. Cyberpunk aesthetic meant to futureproof and empower protesters by styling them in powerful equipment and bold fashions. Exploration of contrast with greyscale/color, traditional/high-tech, flesh/prosthetics, curves/harsh mechanical shapes.(Raymond Lam, Photoshop 2020)
