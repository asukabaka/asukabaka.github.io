AGENTDEPLOY:02 Togo.Hifumi

Paying Homage to the Japanese animated classics Akira and Neon Genesis Evangelion, this piece attempts to explore the edgy and unsettling line between beauty and and mechanical horror of technology. Animated particle effects produced through Vincent Garreau's Javascript Particle System.
(Raymond Lam, Photoshop, Javascript, 2019)
