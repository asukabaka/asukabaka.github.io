JDN S500F

Futuristic design of current day Suzuki GS500F. Special attention given to making the motorcycle and character blend in and become a part of the background. Background made from composited photos of iconic Shibuya Crossing and reflected water textures. Commission for J. Smith of Australia. (Raymond Lam, Photoshop, 2019)
