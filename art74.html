ART74: Intro to Digital Media

San Jose State University. Professor Carrie Hott.

Critical exploration of the history of Digital Media through its variety of mediums, artistic values, along with excercises in practical use.

Glitch Art

After compositing found images on the web into a cyborg, the result is then glitched. Piece is uses audio editing software Audacity, along with photo editing software Photoshop. Glitch Art explores the process of glitching and randomized/machine made art.
(Raymond Lam, Photoshop, Audacity, 2019)

Raster Vector Art

Finding an iconic image to interpret as an emoji, said image is then vectorized. Piece uses Photoshop to make initial adjustments, then Illustrator to vectorize. Raster Vector Art touches upon what makes emojis so prevalent and how hieroglyphics are making a resurgence in modern language.
(Raymond Lam, Photoshop, Illustrator, 2019)

Composite Art

Images on the web related to the theme of night-time cityscape are composited into an image. Photoshop and processes like masking, blending layers and adjustment layers are used extensively to render a surreal image. Composite Art comments on what a heavily militarized and dystopia future might look like.
(Raymond Lam, Photoshop, 2019)

Audacity Composite

Personal recordings of non-related and everyday sounds are collected and edited using Audacity. Audio file is processed through functions like echo, reverb, etc to make an entirely new sound. Audacity Composite attempts to explore aspects of narrative through audio as well, with its increasingly unsettling texture towards the end of the piece.
(Raymond Lam, Audacity, 2019)

Video Audio Composite

Video recordings, along with previously made Audacity Composite project are combined into a video file. Premiere Pro, along with further use of Audacity are utilized in the making of the piece. The movie explores visual storytelling and the role audio plays in the narrative.
(Raymond Lam, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Audacity, 2019)

Net Art

Images are coded with basic HTML and CSS to create an art piece experienced exclusively on a web-browser. Text editor Atom is used, along with Photoshop for the image elements. Piece explores early personal experiences with the internet and the light-hearted and sometimes dark aspects that come along with it.
(Raymond Lam, Photoshop, Atom, 2019)

Code as Art

Piece is coded entirely in Javascript as opposed to HTML and CSS. Referring to the P5JS library, Code Art uses functions like mouse overs and if/else statements. The piece comments on the increasingly invaded privacy of everyday internet users, and the role government and corporations play.
(Raymond Lam, Photoshop, P5JS Web Editor, 2019)

Final Project - Composite Series

Series is a further exploration of compositing images. More advanced techniques of using Photoshop are attempted, through the use of processes such as filters, blending. Special care is used to make images realistic through the application of traditional art theory such as perspective and lighting. Images are a form of photobashing, or digitally brushing upon existing live images, to give a hyper-realistic yet surreal feel.
(Raymond Lam, Photoshop, Illustrator, 2019)
