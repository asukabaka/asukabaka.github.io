ART108 Blog 8 - 808 - A Hall - 4



808 - A Hall - 4 is a game that takes after the indie darling VA-11 Hall-A. Instead of being a bartender, you mix boba drinks for them instead. The game is a hybrid resource management puzzle, slice of life simulator, and visual novel. As customers come in for their order, hold conversations with them, and slowly learn about the world 808 - A Hall - 4 lives in.

Sabrina Chan is the artist, focusing on character and item artwork. Minh brings his background in coding and is the writer for the game. I resume the role of producer for this group, while doing light coding, and focus on background artwork.

So far 808 - A Hall - 4 is going as planned. Sabrina has much of the character artwork done, and they look great! Minh has the drink mixing and resource system coded, which provides a pretty compelling vertical slice of what it's like to mix a drink in game. I have background artwork done for the main counter and am currently looking at integrating the VIDE visual novel engine into our game with the help of Minh.
